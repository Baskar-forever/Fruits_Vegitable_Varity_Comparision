Napa Cabbage

Napa Cabbage is a mild, tender variety often used in Asian cuisine, known for its high water content and nutritional benefits.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin K and calcium, supporting bone health.

Rich in folate, beneficial for pregnancy health.

Supports digestion with its high water and fiber content.

Packed with antioxidants that boost the immune system.

Issues It Helps Solve

Promotes heart health by reducing inflammation.

Aids in weight management due to low-calorie content.

Helps maintain hydration with its high water content.

Supports healthy skin with essential vitamins.
