Stonehead Cabbage

Stonehead Cabbage is a firm, round-headed variety known for its excellent disease resistance and sweet, mild flavor.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin C to support immunity and skin health.

Rich in fiber, aiding digestion and gut health.

Contains antioxidants that help reduce inflammation.

Supports heart health by lowering cholesterol levels.

Issues It Helps Solve

Improves digestion and reduces bloating.

Supports weight management with low-calorie content.

Helps regulate blood sugar levels naturally.

Reduces oxidative stress and supports brain health.
