Brunswick Cabbage

Brunswick Cabbage is a cold-hardy variety known for its dense heads, excellent storage ability, and rich flavor.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin C, boosting the immune system.

Rich in fiber, promoting gut health and digestion.

Loaded with antioxidants that help fight inflammation.

Supports bone health with essential minerals.

Issues It Helps Solve

Regulates blood sugar levels naturally.

Reduces inflammation and supports heart health.

Helps in weight management with low-calorie content.
