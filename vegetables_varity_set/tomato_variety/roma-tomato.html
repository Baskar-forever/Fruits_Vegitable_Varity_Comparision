Roma Tomato

Roma Tomatoes are known for their firm texture, low moisture content, and rich flavor, making them ideal for sauces and pastes.

High in lycopene, reducing the risk of heart disease.

Contains antioxidants that protect against cell damage.

Supports digestive health with fiber content.
