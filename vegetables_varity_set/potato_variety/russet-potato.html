Russet Potato

Russet Potatoes are known for their starchy texture, making them ideal for baking, frying, and mashing.

Health Benefits

Rich in fiber, aiding digestion and gut health.

Good source of vitamin C, supporting immunity.

Provides potassium for heart and muscle health.

Contains antioxidants that promote overall well-being.

Issues It Helps Solve

Regulates blood pressure with high potassium content.

Supports digestion and relieves constipation.

Provides sustained energy with complex carbohydrates.

Boosts immune system with vitamin C and antioxidants.
