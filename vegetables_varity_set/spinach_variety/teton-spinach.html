Teton Spinach

Teton Spinach is a nutrient-rich variety known for its dark green leaves, high resistance to disease, and excellent flavor.

Health Benefits

High in iron, supporting oxygen circulation in the body.

Rich in vitamins A, C, and K for immune support.

Contains antioxidants that promote skin and heart health.

Boosts digestion and gut health with fiber.

Issues It Helps Solve

Supports bone health and reduces osteoporosis risk.

Helps lower blood pressure with potassium content.

Enhances vision and reduces risk of eye diseases.

Prevents iron deficiency and promotes energy levels.
