Kolibri Spinach

Kolibri Spinach is a fast-growing variety known for its dark green leaves, smooth texture, and high disease resistance.

Health Benefits

Excellent source of iron for healthy blood circulation.

Rich in vitamin C to boost immunity.

High in fiber to aid digestion.

Loaded with antioxidants for skin and heart health.
