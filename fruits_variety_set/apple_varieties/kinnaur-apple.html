Kinnaur Apple

Grown in the high altitudes of Himachal Pradesh, Kinnaur apples are known for their natural sweetness, crisp texture, and superior quality.

Health Benefits

Rich in dietary fiber, aiding in digestion and gut health.

Packed with vitamin C, boosting immunity.

Supports heart health by maintaining blood pressure levels.

Provides sustained energy with its natural sugars.

Issues It Helps Solve

Helps in weight management due to its high fiber and low-calorie content.

Reduces the risk of heart diseases by lowering bad cholesterol.

Promotes healthy skin with its antioxidant properties.

Assists in maintaining stable blood sugar levels.
