Red Banana

Red Bananas are sweeter and softer than regular bananas, packed with antioxidants and essential nutrients.

Health Benefits

Rich in antioxidants, promoting overall health.

High in potassium, supporting heart health and blood pressure regulation.

Boosts immune function with high vitamin C content.

Aids digestion and promotes gut health with dietary fiber.

Issues It Helps Solve

Helps reduce blood pressure levels due to high potassium.

Supports digestive health and relieves constipation.

Provides a natural energy boost for active lifestyles.

Helps combat anemia with high iron content.
