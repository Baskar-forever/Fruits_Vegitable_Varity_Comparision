Cavendish Banana

Cavendish Bananas are the most widely consumed variety globally, known for their mild sweetness and creamy texture.

Health Benefits

Rich in potassium, promoting heart health and muscle function.

Excellent source of dietary fiber for improved digestion.

High in natural sugars, providing an instant energy boost.

Packed with vitamins and minerals for overall well-being.

Issues It Helps Solve

Regulates blood pressure due to its potassium content.

Boosts digestion and prevents constipation.

Provides a natural energy source for athletes and active individuals.

Supports mental health by aiding serotonin production.
