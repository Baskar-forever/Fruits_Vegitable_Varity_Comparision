Cara Cara Orange

Cara Cara Oranges have a pinkish-red flesh with a sweet, low-acid flavor, making them a delicious and nutritious citrus choice.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin C, boosting immune health and collagen production.

Rich in lycopene, an antioxidant that supports heart health.

Promotes eye health with vitamin A and carotenoids.

Supports digestion and gut health with natural fiber.

Issues It Helps Solve

Reduces risk of heart disease by lowering oxidative stress.

Enhances skin health by reducing free radical damage.

Aids in maintaining blood sugar levels.

Helps improve digestion and prevents constipation.
