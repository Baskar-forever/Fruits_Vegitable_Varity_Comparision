Tangelo Orange

Tangelo Oranges are a hybrid citrus fruit known for their juicy, tangy-sweet flavor and easy-to-peel skin.

Health Benefits

Rich in vitamin C, helping boost immunity and skin health.

High in fiber, aiding digestion and gut health.

Contains antioxidants that help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

Supports heart health by improving blood circulation and lowering cholesterol.

Issues It Helps Solve

Boosts the immune system, reducing the risk of infections.

Promotes digestive health and prevents constipation.

Hydrates the body due to its high water content.

Helps maintain glowing skin and reduces signs of aging.
