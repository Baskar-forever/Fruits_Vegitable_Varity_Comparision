MD-2 Pineapple

MD-2 Pineapples are known for their perfect balance of sweetness and acidity, high vitamin C content, and long shelf life, making them one of the most popular commercial pineapple varieties.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin C, boosting immune health.

Contains bromelain, aiding digestion and reducing inflammation.

Rich in antioxidants, promoting overall well-being.

Supports hydration and aids in detoxification.

Issues It Helps Solve

Aids in digestion and helps prevent constipation.

Boosts energy levels with natural sugars and nutrients.

Helps reduce inflammation and improve joint health.

Supports cardiovascular health by promoting circulation.
