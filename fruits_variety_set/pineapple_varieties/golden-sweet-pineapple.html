Golden Sweet Pineapple

Golden Sweet Pineapples are prized for their vibrant yellow flesh, exceptionally sweet flavor, and juicy texture, making them a favorite tropical fruit.

Health Benefits

High in vitamin C, boosting immunity and skin health.

Rich in bromelain, aiding digestion and reducing inflammation.

Supports strong bones with its manganese content.

Helps in hydration and provides essential antioxidants.
