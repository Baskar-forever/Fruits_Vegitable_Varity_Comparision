Smooth Cayenne Pineapple

Smooth Cayenne Pineapples are known for their juicy flesh, moderate acidity, and high sugar content, making them a preferred choice for fresh consumption and canning.

Health Benefits

Rich in vitamin C, enhancing immune function.

Contains bromelain, aiding digestion and reducing inflammation.

Supports bone health due to its manganese content.

Helps in hydration and detoxification.
